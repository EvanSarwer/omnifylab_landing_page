Who We Are

Engineering the Future of Industrial Intelligence

OmnifyLab is a research and development focused engineering company specializing in advanced industrial automation, IoT solutions, embedded systems, AI-integrated systems, and custom technology solutions. We work at the intersection of research and real-world deployment to solve complex industrial challenges.

  • We develop end-to-end IoT and embedded system solutions, covering custom hardware, firmware, edge devices, connectivity, dashboards, and data analytics.

Our approach combines deep technical expertise, practical engineering, and a commitment to delivering scalable, secure, and performance-driven solutions.

What does a typical engagement look like?

We start with a discovery sprint, move into design/architecture, then build in two-week iterations with weekly demos and monthly KPI reviews.

How fast can we begin?

Most projects kick off within two weeks of signing. We align stakeholders, finalize scope, and set the delivery calendar before sprint one.

Can you partner with our in-house team?

Yes. We collaborate through shared boards, joint standups, and paired reviews so internal teams stay involved and own the roadmap.

How do you measure success?

We define leading and lagging KPIs up front—activation, retention, conversion, and revenue impact—and report on them every sprint.
